Preprocessing Pipeline

01
Lowercasing

All text normalised to lowercase to eliminate case-based variance (e.g. "WIN" ≡ "win").

02
Noise Removal

Punctuation, special characters, and non-semantic numeric tokens stripped to reduce vocabulary noise.

03
Stopword Removal

Common English stopwords (the, is, at…) removed to reduce dimensionality and focus on discriminative terms.

04
TF-IDF Vectorisation

Cleaned tokens converted into numerical vectors reflecting term frequency balanced by corpus-wide rarity.
