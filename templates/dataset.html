Preprocessing Pipeline

01
Lowercasing

All text normalised to lowercase to eliminate case-based variance (e.g. "WIN" = "win").

↓
02
Noise Removal

Punctuation, special characters, and numeric tokens stripped unless semantically significant.

↓
03
Stopword Removal

Common English stopwords (e.g. "the", "is") removed to reduce dimensionality and focus on discriminative terms.

↓
04
TF-IDF Vectorisation

Cleaned tokens converted into numerical vectors that reflect both term frequency and corpus-wide rarity.
