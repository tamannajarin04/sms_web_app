Preprocessing Steps
  • 1 Normalize label casing: str.lower().str.strip() so "Smishing", "smishing" → "smishing"
  • 2 Map labels to binary target: ham → 0, spam/smishing → 1
  • 3 Drop rows where target is NaN (unknown label variants)
  • 4 Drop URL, EMAIL, PHONE columns — text alone suffices for high accuracy
  • 5 80/20 stratified train-test split to preserve class ratio
  • 6 Fit TF-IDF vectorizer on training set; transform both splits
