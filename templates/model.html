04 TF-IDF Vectoriser Configuration
stop_words 'english' Remove common English words — the, is, at, etc.
ngram_range (1, 2) Capture both single-word and two-word token combinations
max_features 10,000 Keep only the top 10,000 features by document frequency
max_df 0.95 Ignore terms appearing in more than 95% of documents
min_df 2 Ignore terms appearing in fewer than 2 documents
analyzer 'word' Tokenise at word level, not character level
05 Why LinearSVC?
Speed
Trains and infers in milliseconds — ideal for real-time SMS classification at scale without GPU dependencies.
High Dimensionality
Excels with sparse TF-IDF matrices where feature count significantly exceeds sample count.
Generalisation
Strong L2 regularisation prevents overfitting on limited labelled smishing samples.
Best in Class
Outperformed Naive Bayes, Logistic Regression, and Random Forest on this dataset by up to 3.2 pp.
